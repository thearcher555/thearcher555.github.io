Why use Sakura?

Sakura is a decentralized app on the Ethereum blockchain, which runs in a global network upheld by thousands of nodes.

Traditional payrolls face issues with high amounts of intervention needed to send out a correct payroll. Sakura changes this by automatically calculating payment and making transfers cheap and instantaneous.

We aim to make pay roll simple and trustworthy for both workers and companies. With Sakura, employees are guarenteed their pay, and administration of payroll is effortless to upkeep. With an intuitive user experience, Sakura is the solution for making payroll simple.

Decentralized for security and control.

Payroll systems of the past place company funds and employee wages in someone else's hands. With Sakura, you have full control of compensation - direct from employer to employee.

All company data is secured and encrypted by the Ethereum protocol. Your payment plan is verifiable by all employees.

Companies may designate administrators to help govern your payment plans, and streamline moderation. Sakura inherits from, improves, and modernizes the payment plans of today.
